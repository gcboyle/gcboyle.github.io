Introduction

Grateful Bread

At grateful bread, there is no such thing as a lack of appreciation for food. Our team radiates gratitude and love into each and every one of our creations. In the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo, our store opens every beautiful morning from 7 am to 4 pm. From breakfast coffees and croissants to paninis for lunch, we are a full-service daily bakeshop. Famously known for our bread, we offer full loaves and bread-centered meals for sale.

Target audience

Grateful Bread is a welcoming restaurant for all community members, we are family-friendly and encourage people of all ages to come dine with us. Our customers can come to our website to browse the menu prior to arriving or for takeout orders. People can also make reservations and contact us about other inquiries such as location, job opportunities, and more, through our site.

Home

Located in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo in the charming Garden Street district, Grateful Bread offers more than just baked goods. Designed as a boutique convenience bakeshop Grateful Bread features a wide range of breakfast and lunch fare (frittatas, sandwiches, and salads. Using only the finest quality ingredients, Grateful Bread products focus on quality and freshness, including vegan and gluten-free options.
